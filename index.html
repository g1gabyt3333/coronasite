Covid-19 Information

What is Covid-19?

Coronavirus is a respiratory disease caused by the 2019-Sars-Cov2 virus. It was first reported in China in late 2019. It spreads through droplets that contain virus particles

Common symptoms of Covid-19 include:

  1. Fever
  2. Coughing
  3. Shortness of Breath
  4. Fatigue

If you are experiencing any of these symptoms you should call your doctor and get tested. If you are expericing shortness of breath go to the hospital immediately!

What can we do to prevent it? Covid-19 can be prevented in many different ways!

These include:
  1. Masks
  2. Washing Hands
  3. Staying indoors unless necessary
  4. Minimizing contact with people outside your immediate family
